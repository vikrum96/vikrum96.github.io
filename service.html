Service

I have participated in different hackathons and competitions and through these events, I have collaborated with various teams and applied my skills to intriguing projects. I would like to give back by helping even the younger individuals who perhaps don't know what they should do in a hackathon. These individuals could be discouraged to participate due to them thinking they are not adequate enough and because to that, I would like to, in the future, help these individuals in any way I could as I was once in their shoes.

While I have yet to dive into things like educating people, this is also an area I would like to contribute to. Creating workshops based on things like basic machine learning concepts or data visualization techniques for beginners and people who are new to the space, I feel would be very helpful. These "hard-to-approach" concepts will be made easier by doing this and as a result, the field as a whole will innevitably grow, even if only by a little bit by this influence.

These aspirations are in-line with my overarching goal of utilizing my technical skills to bring out the creativity within not only myself but the individuals around me.
